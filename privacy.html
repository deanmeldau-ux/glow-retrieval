Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  |  Glow Retrieval, Cape Town, South Africa

This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA). We take your privacy and your clients' privacy seriously.

1. Who This Policy Covers

This policy applies to:

  • Prospective clients who visit our website or contact us
  • Active clients who have purchased a Glow Retrieval service
  • Lead data provided by clients for the purpose of follow-up sequences

2. What Data We Collect

From you (the business owner):

  • Your name, business name, email address, and WhatsApp number
  • Business details provided in the onboarding form (services, pricing, escalation contacts)
  • Communication history (emails, messages, approvals)
  • Payment records (processed by Stripe - we do not store card details)

From the lead lists you provide:

  • Names, phone numbers, and/or email addresses of people who previously enquired
  • Original enquiry context (if included in your export)
  • Response status and opt-out records

From website visitors:

  • Standard web analytics (page views, referral source) via GitHub Pages hosting - no cookies placed by us

3. Why We Collect It

  • To deliver the lead recovery service you paid for
  • To draft, send, and track follow-up messages on your behalf
  • To generate weekly reports
  • To process payments and issue invoices
  • To communicate with you about your account

We do not use your data or your leads' data for any other purpose.

4. How We Store It

Lead data is stored in secure, access-controlled files on South African or EU-based systems. We do not store data on public servers or share cloud drives. Payment data is handled entirely by Stripe and subject to Stripe's privacy policy.

5. Who We Share It With

Nobody. We do not sell, rent, share, or disclose your data or your leads' data to any third party, except:

  • Stripe (payment processing only)
  • As required by South African law or a valid legal order

6. How Long We Keep It

  • Active client data: retained for the duration of the service plus 12 months for invoicing and legal purposes
  • Lead lists: deleted within 7 days of service cancellation or written request
  • Opt-out records: retained indefinitely to ensure compliance
  • Payment records: retained for 5 years as required by South African tax law

7. Your Rights Under POPIA

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention obligations)
  • Object to processing of your personal information
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@glowretrieval.com. We will respond within 10 business days.

8. Lead Data & Third-Party Consent

You, the client, are responsible for ensuring that the leads you provide to us made their enquiry via an opt-in or public channel. By submitting a lead list, you confirm that you have a lawful basis to follow up with those contacts under POPIA. We will not follow up with any contact who did not initiate an enquiry.

9. Opt-Outs

Any lead who requests to stop receiving messages is removed immediately and permanently. Opt-out records are kept indefinitely to prevent re-contact. If a lead contacts you directly to opt out, please notify us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Active clients will be notified by email. The updated policy will always be available at glowretrieval.com/privacy.html.

11. Contact

Privacy questions or data requests: hello@glowretrieval.com

Information Regulator of South Africa: www.inforegulator.org.za

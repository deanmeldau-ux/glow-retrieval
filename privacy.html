Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  |  Glow Retrieval, Cape Town, South Africa

This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the rights of everyone involved under the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA). We take the privacy of homeowners and providers seriously.

1. Who This Policy Covers

  • Homeowners who submit an enquiry on one of our niche sites (for example DryCape)
  • Service providers who apply for or receive leads from us
  • Visitors to glowretrieval.com and our niche sites

2. What Data We Collect

From homeowners who enquire:

  • Name, contact number and/or email address
  • Their suburb and a description of the work they need (for example "rising damp in a Constantia home")

From providers:

  • Your name, business name, email address and WhatsApp number
  • The trade and suburbs you cover, and your chosen plan
  • Communication history and payment records (payments processed by our payment provider; we do not store card details)

From website visitors:

  • Standard analytics such as page views and referral source, used to improve the sites and measure where enquiries come from

3. Why We Collect It

  • To connect a homeowner with one suitable local provider
  • To route exclusive leads to the correct provider and track coverage
  • To operate provider accounts, process payments and issue invoices
  • To improve the ranking and content of our niche sites

We do not use this data for any other purpose, and we do not sell it.

4. The Homeowner Enquiry and Consent

When a homeowner submits an enquiry on one of our forms, they are asking to be connected with a local specialist. By submitting the form they consent to their details being shared with one provider for the purpose of responding to that enquiry. We make this clear on the form itself. We share their details with one provider only.

5. What Providers May Do With Homeowner Data

A provider who receives a lead may use those details only to respond to that specific enquiry. Providers may not resell the data, add the homeowner to marketing lists, or pass the details to anyone else. If a homeowner asks not to be contacted, the provider must stop immediately and tell us.

6. How We Store It

Enquiry and provider data is held in secure, access-controlled systems. Payment data is handled by our payment provider and is subject to their privacy policy. We do not place enquiry data on public or shared drives.

7. Who We Share It With

We share a homeowner's enquiry with the single provider routed to receive it. Beyond that we do not sell, rent or disclose data to any third party, except:

  • Our payment provider (for payment processing only)
  • Where required by South African law or a valid legal order

8. How Long We Keep It

  • Homeowner enquiries: retained while we manage the introduction, then for a limited period for quality and dispute purposes
  • Provider account data: retained for the duration of the relationship plus 12 months for invoicing and legal purposes
  • Opt-out requests: retained as needed to ensure they are honoured
  • Payment records: retained for 5 years as required by South African tax law

9. Your Rights Under POPIA

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, to request correction or deletion (subject to legal retention), to object to processing, and to lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa. To exercise any of these rights, contact hello@glowretrieval.com and we will respond within a reasonable period.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version will always be available at glowretrieval.com/privacy.html.

11. Contact

Privacy questions or data requests: hello@glowretrieval.com

Information Regulator of South Africa: www.inforegulator.org.za

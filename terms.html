1. Who We Are

Glow Retrieval is a lead recovery service based in Cape Town, South Africa. We help businesses follow up with old, unanswered enquiries using approved messaging templates and weekly reporting. We operate via hello@glowretrieval.com.

2. What We Do

We provide a done-for-you missed-lead recovery system. Specifically, we:

  • Import and clean a list of old leads provided by the client
  • Draft follow-up message sequences tailored to the client's business
  • Obtain the client's written approval before sending any message

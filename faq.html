During the Service

If a lead replies with interest - asks a question, requests a quote, or shows any intent to book - we escalate it to you immediately by email. You get their name, contact details, and the message they sent. From that point, you handle the conversation directly. We do not close bookings on your behalf.

If a lead replies to opt out, we remove them immediately and you are notified in your next weekly report.

Every Friday you receive a report showing:

  • Total leads in your sequence
  • How many have been contacted
  • How many have replied
  • How many have been escalated to you as hot leads
  • How many have been marked inactive (no response after full sequence)
  • Opt-outs recorded that week

Each lead receives a maximum of two messages:

  • Day 0: The initial recovery message, sent on a weekday between 09:00 and 16:00.
  • Day 5: A single follow-up if there has been no reply.
  • Day 10: If still no reply, the lead is marked inactive and no further contact is made.

We never send more than two messages to any lead. We never contact leads on weekends.

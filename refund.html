Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026  |  Glow Retrieval, Cape Town, South Africa

We built our refund policy before we built our pricing. If we don't deliver, you don't pay. That's not a marketing line - it's written here, and it applies automatically.

How to Get a Refund

In most cases, you don't have to do anything. If a refund trigger is met (see below), we initiate it. You don't need to chase us, argue, or fill in a form. If for any reason a refund isn't processed within 5 business days of the trigger date, email hello@glowretrieval.com and we'll fix it same day.

Refund Triggers by Tier

Pilot - R1,500 once-off

Trigger: We have not delivered a complete follow-up sequence (templates approved, messages sent, weekly report in your inbox) by Day 14 from the date your onboarding form was completed.

What happens: You owe nothing. We do not issue an invoice. If you have already paid and we miss this deadline, we refund the full R1,500 with no deduction.

Timing: Refund initiated within 2 business days of Day 14 if the trigger is met.

Standard - R2,500/month

Trigger: Your first full calendar month includes zero follow-up activity from Glow Retrieval (no messages sent, no report delivered).

What happens: That month's fee is refunded in full.

Cancellation: You may cancel at any time with no notice period. No refund is provided for months where work was delivered. Cancellations after the 1st of a billing month are not refunded for that month if work has already commenced.

Timing: Refund processed within 5 business days of the month-end trigger being confirmed.

Pro - R4,500/month

Trigger: We fail to respond to a client escalation within 4 business hours (08:00–18:00 SAST, Monday to Friday) in any given calendar month.

What happens: A 50% credit (R2,250) is applied to your next invoice automatically. This is a credit, not a cash refund, unless you cancel - in which case it is paid out.

Cancellation: Same as Standard. No notice period. No lock-in.

Timing: Credit applied on next invoice. If cancelling, paid out within 5 business days.

What Is Not Covered

Refunds do not apply when:

  • The client has not completed the onboarding form, which is required before work can start
  • The client has not approved message templates, which are required before messages can be sent
  • The pilot delay was caused by the client (e.g. no lead list provided, no template approval)
  • The client simply changed their mind and work was already delivered
  • Leads did not respond - this is a lead quality issue, not a service delivery failure
  • You requested a refund for a month where reports, messages, and escalation handling were all delivered

How Refunds Are Paid

Refunds are returned to the original payment method (Stripe). Processing time depends on your card or bank - typically 3 to 5 business days after we initiate the refund.

Disputes

If you believe a refund is owed and we have not initiated it, contact us at hello@glowretrieval.com. We will review and respond within 2 business days. If we cannot resolve the dispute directly, it will be referred to mediation under South African law before any further steps.

Questions

Email hello@glowretrieval.com - Ryan will get back to you within a few hours.

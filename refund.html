We want you buying real work, not noise. This policy explains when a lead is credited back, how the suburb retainer works, and how cancellations and refunds are handled. It is plain and it applies the same way for everyone.

The Simple Version

If we send you a lead that is clearly not a genuine enquiry, you do not pay for it. We credit it back. If you hold a suburb on a monthly retainer, you can stop any time with no notice period. Unused prepaid lead credits stay on your account.

Pay Per Lead Credits

When a lead is credited back

We credit a lead back to your balance when it is clearly not genuine, namely:

1. The contact details are fake or do not work (wrong number, dead email, no such person).
2. It is spam, a test, or an obvious duplicate of a lead you already received.
3. It is plainly outside the suburb or service you signed up for.
4. The enquiry is from a renter with no authority or budget to commission the work.
5. The homeowner is unreachable after reasonable attempts to make contact.

How to claim: tell us within 48 hours of receiving the lead, with a short note on what was wrong. We review quickly and credit valid claims back to your balance.

Unused credits

Prepaid lead credits do not expire while your account is active. If you decide to stop, any unused credit can be refunded to your original payment method on request, less any leads already delivered.

Suburb Exclusive Retainer

Cancellation

You may cancel a suburb retainer at any time. There is no notice period and no cancellation fee. Your exclusivity runs to the end of the month you have already paid for, then stops, and the suburb becomes available to other providers.

If we deliver nothing

If a full paid month passes with zero qualifying enquiries delivered to you in your suburb, that month's retainer is refunded in full. You should not pay for a month in which we delivered no leads at all. (Note: damp demand is seasonal and quieter in summer, which is why we also rank year-round services, but if a month is truly empty, it is on us.)

What Is Not Covered

Credits and refunds do not apply when:

  • The lead was genuine but the homeowner chose another quote, went quiet, or decided not to proceed (winning the job is up to you)
  • You did not respond to the lead in good time
  • You claim after the 48 hour window for pay per lead credits
  • You simply changed your mind about a lead that was real and reachable
  • A retainer month did deliver qualifying enquiries, even if fewer than you hoped
  • A homeowner dispute relates to your quote, workmanship or guarantee, which is between you and the homeowner

How Refunds Are Paid

Approved cash refunds are returned to your original payment method. Processing time depends on your bank, typically 3 to 5 business days after we initiate the refund. Lead credit claims are applied to your account balance immediately on approval.

Disputes

If you believe a credit or refund is owed and we have not actioned it, contact hello@glowretrieval.com. We review and respond within 2 business days. If we cannot resolve it directly, it will be referred to mediation under South African law before any further steps.

Questions

Email hello@glowretrieval.com and we will get back to you within a few hours on weekdays.

Common Questions

THE THINGS PEOPLE
ACTUALLY WANT TO KNOW.

What if none of my old leads reply?
That's a real possibility - and it's why the pilot exists. We're not promising you bookings. We're promising a working, professional recovery system that gives those leads a second chance. If the system runs and nobody replies, that tells you something valuable: those leads were truly dead. Either way, you have proof. You don't pay unless we deliver the system itself.
My leads are 6 months old. Is it too late?
Probably not. A well-timed, professional message to someone who enquired 6 months ago and just hasn't booked yet still converts - especially for considered purchases like aesthetic treatments, dental work, or home renovations. The window is wider than most businesses think. We'll tell you upfront if the list looks genuinely too old to be worth running.
Who actually sends the messages - a robot or a person?
Messages are drafted by our team, approved by you before first use, and sent from a managed account. They read like a human wrote them - because a human did. We never claim to be clinical staff, never fabricate information, and never send anything you haven't seen and signed off on first.
What access do you need to my accounts?
None. You give us an export of your old leads (a WhatsApp chat export, a spreadsheet, an email thread dump - whatever you have). We do the rest from our own systems. We never log into your WhatsApp, Instagram, or email accounts. Your logins and your business accounts stay yours.
What if I want to stop?
Message us and we stop. On Standard and Pro there's no notice period and no cancellation fee. We delete your lead data within 7 days of your request. We'd rather earn your business every month than trap you into staying.
Why start with the pilot instead of going straight to Standard?
You shouldn't sign up for a monthly service from a company you've never worked with. The pilot is designed to prove we're real, we're professional, and the system works - before you commit to anything ongoing. R1,500 is the cost of finding out. Most clients recover that in one booking.
